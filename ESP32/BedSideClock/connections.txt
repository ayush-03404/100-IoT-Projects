ESP32 Weather Display & Relay Control - Connections

1. OLED Display (SSD1306 I2C)
--------------------------------
- VCC  → 3.3V (ESP32)
- GND  → GND (ESP32)
- SDA  → GPIO 21 (ESP32)
- SCL  → GPIO 22 (ESP32)

2. Capacitive Touch Sensor (TTP223 or similar)
----------------------------------------------
- VCC  → 3.3V or 5V (ESP32)
- GND  → GND (ESP32)
- OUT  → GPIO 4 (ESP32)

3. Relay Module
----------------
- VCC  → 5V (ESP32 or external 5V supply)
- GND  → GND (ESP32)
- IN   → GPIO 5 (ESP32)

Notes:
------
- Ensure all GNDs are common (OLED, ESP32, Touch Sensor, Relay).
- The touch sensor outputs HIGH when pressed; this is read by ESP32.
- Relay is activated when GPIO pin is set HIGH (depending on module type, some modules are active LOW).
- Use short jumper wires for reliable connections to the breadboard or ESP32.
